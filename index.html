Digital Garden

Venus is the goddess for love, art and beauty.
Lilith is the first woman who was cursed for standing up to pathriarchy and female sexuality.
These two female archetypes works as a core to the visual expression.
This project was born from the desire to explore the website as a garden.
Creativity becomes a metaphor to growing and objects are added as new ideas come up.

A graduation project of Johanna Lundberg with music by Lithe & Free.
This is a digital freezone for visual exploration in physical and digital materiality.

Typeface: Masuria by Blaze Type
