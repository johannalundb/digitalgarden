Digital Garden

This is the graduation project of Johanna Lundberg with music by Lithe & Free. The objects and background are made of synthetic material, like 3D-graphics and manipulated stock photos to create an artificial experience of nature in a digital format. CSS-animations and different scroll speeds creates an illusion of depth and weight, while music play different sounds depending on your position.

This project was born from the desire to create a free space for visual exploration and creativity. The garden grows as new ideas come.

Typeface: Masuria by Blaze Type
